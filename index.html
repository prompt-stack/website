FAQ

Do I need to know how to code?

No. But you should want to build things. If you tinker, automate, and ship ‚Äî you'll feel at home. This isn't a tool for passive consumption.

What does it cost?

Free during beta. After that, $199/year. You pay your AI provider separately for API usage.

Where does my data go?

Your files stay on your machine. Conversations go to your AI provider (that's how AI works). PromptStack doesn't store or collect your data. Telemetry is opt-in only.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a chat window. You talk, it responds, you copy-paste the output somewhere else. PromptStack is a workstation ‚Äî it sees your files, builds automations, runs them, and saves everything.

How is this different from Cursor?

Cursor is an IDE for software engineers who write code. PromptStack is a workstation for builders who direct AI. You don't need to know TypeScript. You need to know what you want.

How is this different from Lovable?

Lovable is great for quick prototypes. But you can't see all the code, can't reference your own files, can't control the architecture. When you're ready to build real systems ‚Äî not demos ‚Äî that's PromptStack.

How is this different from n8n?

n8n is node-based automation. You wire boxes together. PromptStack is AI-native ‚Äî you describe what you want, the AI figures out the wiring. Less configuration, more conversation.

Is it safe?

PromptStack has pre-configured safety measures. You can adjust them. We recommend RUDI (Responsible Use of Digital Intelligence) principles.
